1.WHO IS GOKU

Goku is the main hero of the Dragon Ball anime and manga series created by Akira Toriyama. His full Saiyan name is Kakarot. He was born on Planet Vegeta and sent to Earth as a baby. He is kind-hearted, loves to fight strong opponents, and always protects his friends and the Earth. Goku is a Saiyan, a race of powerful warriors. He can transform into stronger forms like Super Saiyan, Super Saiyan Blue, Ultra Instinct, etc. He learned martial arts from Master Roshi, trained with King Kai, and fights famous villains like Frieza, Cell, Majin Buu, and Jiren.
